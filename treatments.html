Pain GPS Clinic emphasizes comprehensive care with a multidisciplinary perspective.

We offer complete evaluation, treatment recommendations, coordination of care, and alternative approaches when usual options fail.

Common Pain Conditions

  • Musculoskeletal Pain
  • Joint Pain
  • Face Pain
  • Neck/Cervical Pain
  • Back Pain
  • Shoulder, Arm & Elbow Pain
  • Hip & Leg Pain
  • Pelvic Pain
  • Knee Pain
  • Foot Pain
  • Cancer Pain
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Neuropathic Pain
  • And less common conditions such as CRPS and Erythromelalgia

Common Procedures and Treatments

  • Medication Management
  • Trigger Point Injections
  • Joint Steroid Injection(s)
  • Intramuscular Medication Injections
  • Nerve Blocks
  • Lidocaine Infusions
  • Ketamine Infusions

  • Behavioral Health (Dialectical Behavioral Therapy, EMDR)
